About Me

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics, Engineering, and Computer Science at West Virginia State University. I hold a Ph.D. in Computer and Information Sciences from Temple University. My research focus is on Data Science, Spatial Epidemiology, Natural Language Processing, and Machine Learning/Deep learning.

I am a passionate educator, enjoy teaching, and actively engage in research. I am always open to collaboration with students and researchers who share an interest in these areas.
